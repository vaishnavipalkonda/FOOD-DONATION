About us

Every day, millions sleep on an empty stomach. Help us fill them up

Food is a basic right. And yet, over 190 million people in India do not have food to eat. And thousands of others do not have access to tasty wholesome meals.

The COVID19 pandemic has intensified this situation by pushing millions of people into poverty.

Our Mission: End hunger

A nutritious meal is not something anyone should be denied of. Freedom from hunger is the first step towards empowerment. We believe that no one should have to worry about how to put food on the table.

FEED INDIA aims to contribute towards solving Karnataka’s food crisis.

Our volunteers are dedicated to providing cooked meals to those who in need. Our goal is to ensure that no one sleeps hungry, and everyone has access to a healthy meal.

Backed by the Arya Ved Trust, along with other corporate partners, the FEED INDIA program is already serving tasty, healthy meals to thousands of people across Karnataka.
